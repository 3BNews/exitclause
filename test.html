About Exit Clause

EXIT CLAUSE is a live sci‑fi thriller series following a national digital response unit tasked with investigating unexplained system failures threatening critical infrastructure.

Combining suspense, technology, and real-time broadcast storytelling, the show explores what happens when the failsafe designed to protect society begins acting on its own.
